
<bold><gold>Advanced Features of Lifesteal Utils</gold></bold>

You're using Lifesteal Utils (LSU), a utility mod for Lifesteal Network.

LSU contains optional networking features such as alliance sharing between users. These aren't possible without a centralized service (LSU's Gaia!)

<bold><gold>How does this affect me?</gold></bold>

If you opt in, your client will communicate with LSU's servers in addition to LSN's. This is similar to being on two Minecraft servers at once.

<bold><gold>Why this notice?</gold></bold>

LSU does not act on your behalf without consent.

This consent can be granted / revoked at any time in the future by using /lsu consent-gaia
