## Why do modern alliances have more features?
Modern alliances go through LSU's servers, which allows for secure communication between LSU users. This means we can add more complex features that aren't possible without a central server.

## Why don't local alliances have these features?
Physically not possible. Local alliances are as powerful as we can make them without LSU's servers. We cannot share alliance data automatically between users without a central server.

## Why not just make all alliances modern?
Preferences. Some users may opt to never use LSU's networking services, and they should still have access to whatever benefits LSU can bring them, as previously.

## LSU's servers? Is this secure? This doesn't like steal my token righttt
LSU's networking works on the same principles as any Minecraft server. Just like when you join any minecraft server like LSN, we don't get access to your token or private data when you connect.

LSU uses the same exact system. 

Privacy-wise, LSU's servers are comparable to Minecraft servers. Your computer shares the same amount of information as it does with any minecraft server when you join, like LSN, LSP, Hypixel, etc.

we don't share any data about individuals! unlike some services!

## Is my computer talking with other players' computers? is this going to leak my ip
No. Your computer will only ever talk with LSU's servers. Just like when you're on LSN, your computer talks to LSN's servers, not other players.
